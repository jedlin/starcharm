Do the stars influence your future? Many people know their ˜birth sign¨— the sector of the Zodiac, like Taurus, Virgo, or Scorpio, that is ascendant at the time of their birth. But astrologers believe determining the influence of the stars on a person's life and future involves a far more subtle and elaborate calculation than merely the Zodiac sign. It involves planets, the sun, the moon, and other heavenly bodies and their relationships to one another.

The collection of these astrological factors can be illustrated in a ¨Natal Chart¨—a chart of the positions and relationships of heavenly bodies as seen from the precise place on Earth of someone's birth at the very moment of their birth. As the celestial bodies spin in an ever-changing ballet across the firmament, their specific configuration for a given time and birthplace, captured in a Natal Chart, is almost as unique to an individual as their fingerprint.

StarCharm can engrave this unique pattern, bound to a person's birth, onto a lovely piece of jewelry—a pendant, earrings, a bracelet, a charm, a pin, a ring, or a key fob—they can keep with them always.
